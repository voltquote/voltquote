Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026
VoltQuote is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only what we need to run the service and never sell your data.

1. What We Collect

When you use VoltQuote we collect:

  • Account information (email, company name, phone number)
  • Quote data you generate (job descriptions, estimates)
  • Usage data (how often you use the app, features used)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never see your card number)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the VoltQuote service
  • To send account-related emails (receipts, updates)
  • To improve AI estimate accuracy using anonymized aggregate data
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

3. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your quote data with other users
  • We do not use your data for advertising

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) with industry-standard encryption. Quote data is stored on your device using browser localStorage and optionally in our secure database when you create an account.

5. Third Party Services

VoltQuote uses the following third party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — processes your job descriptions to generate estimates. See Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com
  • Stripe — handles payment processing. See Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com
  • Supabase — stores account data. See Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com
  • Vercel — hosts the application. See Vercel's privacy policy at vercel.com

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@voltquote.net. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Cookies

VoltQuote uses browser localStorage to save your settings and quotes locally. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service grows. We will notify users of significant changes via email.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at support@voltquote.net
