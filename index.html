FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. VoltQuote is built for electricians, not developers. If you can fill out a form, you can use VoltQuote.

How accurate are the quotes?

The AI is trained on real electrical job pricing and uses current 2025–2026 material and labor rates. You can always edit any line item before sending.

Can I send quotes directly to customers?

Yes — with one click you can email a professional PDF quote directly to your customer from inside the app.

Can I export to Excel?

Yes. Every quote can be exported to Excel with one click — perfect if you already run your business on spreadsheets.

What job types does it support?

Panel upgrades, service changes, EV charger installs, rewires, outlet/switch work, lighting, generators, and more. New job types are added regularly.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your quotes and customer data are private to your account and never shared with anyone.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel in one click from your settings page.
